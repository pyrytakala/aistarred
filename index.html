Conference lineups increasingly favor sponsor showcases and polished narratives over hard-won lessons. Plenty of talks restate what you already know, demo a product, or dress marketing up as insight. This list is an attempt to float the ones that still teach something.

How quality is scored

Each transcript is read and scored by an LLM on five dimensions (0–10): substance, evidence, specificity, insight density, and non-promotion.

The composite (0–100) is a weighted sum — substance ×3, evidence ×2, specificity ×1.5, insight ×2.5, non-promotion ×1 — with a small adjustment for relative like count. Click any score badge for a per-talk breakdown.
