How it works

endslop surfaces the top third of videos from selected events and channels — the ones most likely to be worth your time.

What you see

Each list shows only the highest-scoring third of videos we evaluated. Lower-scoring videos are not listed. Rankings, scores, and summary bullets are shown for the picks only.

How quality is scored

Each video is scored by an LLM on five dimensions (0–10): substance, evidence, specificity, insight density, and non-promotion.

The composite (0–100) is a weighted sum — substance ×3, evidence ×2, specificity ×1.5, insight ×2.5, non-promotion ×1 — with a small adjustment for relative like count. Click any score badge on a listing page for a per-video breakdown.

Why this exists

Conferences and podcasts publish a lot of great material, but lineups also include sponsor showcases, recap filler, and content that restates common knowledge. This site tries to highlight the videos that are most likely to repay your attention.

Disclaimer

Scores and summaries are generated automatically by an LLM. They reflect automated, subjective estimates — not objective facts or endorsements. Summaries may be inaccurate or incomplete.
